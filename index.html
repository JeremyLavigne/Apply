Tell us more about you.

My name is Jeremy, 30 years old and father of two boys. I lived my whole life in France, since last year when we moved in Stockholm with my wife.
Best decision we ever take!

Postman, teacher and now developer?

I believe in opportunities, not in one life/one job:

  • A summer job of postman turned into a six-year job,
  • A return to university lead me to a teacher career,
  • A personal change of life (and country) offers me a new start.

Why web developer?

I have always been interested in programming : it is a sweet mix of math and logic, like a puzzle you have to solve.
It often stayed in the background, today I want to put it in the frontground.

Web is attractive because it offers easy access and immediate rendering.

What are your skills/knowledge?

HTML/CSS/JS, at the level you can see here :

  • Responsive web principles (flexbox or bootstrap),
  • Basic user interactions

A little more :

I am currently learning how to use React.

On the Internet, using learning plateform :

My University classes for the algorithmic part and the global vision.

What kind of position are you looking for?

Ideally, web developer. Be part of a development team, learn the best practices in real conditions, with professionals.

Honestly, not immediatly. I am qualified to learn fast and understand when you explain me something. I can take my responsibilities quickly and work by myself as soon as possible.

Can you imagine a different position?

Totally, I understand a team/company have several needs, and all tasks are useful. Enter in this world is my main motivation, whatever the entry door.

Absolutely. I am ready to do what it needs. A different statut/ a different salary seems fair as long as I am not "a real developer".

What if we absolutely need some skill you do not have?

Please tell me which one. Today I am focusing on React, but if you have others conditions, let me try to do my best.

In the best case, it can prepare an interview or a start. Otherwise it will develop my skills and help me anyway.
